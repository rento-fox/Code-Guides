### Title Case Style for Headings

#### Capitalize:
- First and last words
- All major words (nouns, verbs, adjectives, adverbs, pronouns)

#### Do not capitalize:
- articles (a, an, the)
- conjunctions (for, and, nor, but, or, yet, so)
- prepositions (to, for, of, with, in, on, by, at, about)
